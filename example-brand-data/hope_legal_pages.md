# HOPE Legal & Customer Information

# RETURNS & EXCHANGES

If you are not satisfied with an item purchased from our online store, you may return it within 14 days of delivery, provided that it is unworn, unwashed, undamaged, and in the same condition as when you received it. For exchanges, simply return the item and place a new order.

You do not qualify for our return policy if:

- More than 14 days have passed since you received your order.
- Damage or harm has been caused to a product while examining it.
- The product has been broken or damaged and is not in the same condition as when delivered.
- The product’s tags have been removed.

## Returning Items on Sale

Items purchased on sale and shipped outside the EU are not eligible for return unless faulty.

Customers in the EU, EEA (including Norway, Iceland, and Liechtenstein), and the UK have a statutory right to withdraw from a sale purchase within 14 days.

Customers in China and South Korea have the right to return sale items within 7 days after receiving their order.

We do not offer price adjustments for items purchased at full price that later go on sale.

## Returns Within Sweden

For returns within Sweden, you will receive a return form and a PostNord return label with your order.

### For PostNord Returns

1. Fill in the return form.
2. Place the item(s) you want to return in the plastic bag your order came in, or in any other suitable packaging.
3. Attach the PostNord return label to the package.
4. Take the parcel to your nearest PostNord delivery point.

**Return fee:** 70 SEK

## Returns for All Other Countries

The return shipping cost is the customer’s responsibility, and HOPE does not provide return labels. You may use any carrier of your choice.

### Returns Outside the EU

Please note that additional duties, taxes, or any other charges incurred will not be refunded by HOPE.

Mark the goods as “RETURN” on the customs invoice and declare the correct value of the items. If the value is declared as zero, the shipment may be delayed or returned to the sender.

## Return In Store

You can return your items at our Stockholm SoFo store located at Götgatan 34 in Stockholm, or at our Gothenburg store located at Södra Larmgatan 13.

Returns made in-store are free of charge. Simply bring your item(s) along with the return form. Once your return has been processed, you will receive a confirmation email.

## Refunds

Your refund will be credited to the original payment method used for the order.

Returns and refunds are processed as soon as possible after the returned items have been received and registered at our warehouse in Stockholm. The process may take up to 30 days to complete.

Please note that the original shipping cost is not refunded.

## Unclaimed Packages

All unclaimed packages will be returned to us. Any fees associated with the return of an unclaimed package will be charged to the customer.

## Guarantees

HOPE complies with all rules and regulations issued by the Swedish Consumer Agency.
